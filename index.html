About

Data, research, and a passion for disability rights.

I'm a published autism researcher. My work in journals like Autism and Exceptional Children looks at how we write about autistic people and how their education goals get set.

At the U.S. Department of Education, I steward Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) Section 618 data for students with disabilities: I run the Discipline and Personnel collections, co-produce and co-publish the Office of Special Education Programs (OSEP) Fast Facts, and develop accessible, internal-use Section 508-compliant dashboards, increasingly powered by AI tech. Alongside my federal role, I founded and lead the Autism and Neurodivergency Advocacy Association, a nonprofit serving the neurodivergent community. (Views my own, not my employer's.)

At the U.S. Department of Education, I take care of national data about students with disabilities. I help collect it, check that it is correct, share it with the public in easy-to-read reports, and build tools that make it usable for everyone. 2024 to now
current role

Data Analyst (Education Program Specialist)

U.S. Department of Education, Office of Special Education Programs

I serve as the data steward and subject-matter expert for Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) Section 618 reporting: the federal data that every U.S. state submits each year on the infants, children, and students it serves in special education. My work spans six national data collections that shape how the country understands and funds services for students with disabilities. (Views my own, not my employer's.)

  • Run the IDEA Discipline (2023–24, 2024–25) and Personnel (2024–25) collections and their static tables. In plain terms: the Discipline data tracks how often students with disabilities are suspended, expelled, or removed from class, and the Personnel data counts the special-education teachers and staff who serve them. Together they show how students with disabilities are served nationwide.
  • The collections I work on affect 8M+ students with disabilities: I assist regularly on the Assessment collection (state-test performance) and on Maintenance of Effort and Coordinated Early Intervening Services (MOE/CEIS), and have previously assisted with the Exiting, Child Count, and Educational Environments collections.
  • Co-authored the four most recent OSEP Fast Facts releases, turning IDEA Section 618 data into accessible, Section 508-compliant data stories for the public.
